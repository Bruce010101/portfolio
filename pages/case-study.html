summary

An application that will create a more straight
forward and honest environment. Made to help
jobseekers and HR people as a whole. Giving
information in fields it once lacked.

role
ux/ui designer

problem definition

The Project started naturally with a brief from our client. He briefly explained to us his goal and vision, and also explained his blockers and issues regarding the project. We questioned him additionally to gain further information and to make the process easier to work on.

research

After the breakdown, the user wanted to have a product in Macedonia that is similar to glassdoor, where people can check the salary for a specific work position and also to be able to view the benefits of working and the work environment. The user should be able to enter the site and provide information about his salary. The information shared is anonymous and he is able to share information about the work environment, the pros & cons and also provide a rating for the company.

user interviews

We started with analyzing the competitors, and determining the main obstacles that we need to work around in order to make the product a success. The main problem was to be easy to use and in the same time for the user not to be overwhelmed with information. Afterwards I researched the competitors and did a detailed competitor analysis. Afterwards we created the personas, one primary and one secondary, and we conducted a survey of questions regarding the product. From the research and interviews I concluded that we need to work on a clean and easy to use design, given the fact that HR reps and Job seekers have pretty busy schedules, and need to find their required information quickly.

analytics

The Analytics showed that the users preferred a mobile first design given the fact that today everything is created with mobile first in mind. Also, from the research I conducted that a min max range for the pay would be widely accepted for a position, and a search tab where the users can search for salaries for a specific position.

solution

After the analysis of the competitors, user interviews, target groups the solution would be to create a mobile first easy to use and user-friendly product where the information regarding salaries and work environments are just a couple of clicks away.

outcome

Firstly, we finished with the wireframes, afterwards we choose the colors of the Brainster Wortech page. To give the companies a personal touch we added the Wortech logo on the left and on the right, we added the call-to-action button where users can enter salaries for specific work positions. We kept the whole design clean and added textures in the background to distinguish the product from the competitors. We also added the top paying positions and top companies who are rated by users, also we added the option for the user to run a test in order for him to find out his career path. And last but not least we added the option for users to compare companies.

learnings

After presenting the finished product, having worked in a team for the first time on a real project, I learned a lot about myself, as well as about the design principles. The biggest lesson would be that the saying “Less is more” does not always apply. Meaning we were focused on making the process of providing a salary easy to use, when in reality it requires more information to be provided and therefore more clicks on the users end.
