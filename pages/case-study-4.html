project

SUGARWISH (WINNING SOLUTION)

summary

A challenge where we made an already good
experience even better and engage the customer
throughout the whole experience.

role
ux/ui designer

problem definition

As usual the discovery phase began with a brief session from our clients. They explained to us their goals and visions, and also explained their blockers and issues regarding the project. We then asked additional questions in order to gain further information and clarify the whole process. This process was a whole different experience, but more on that later on.

research

After the briefing session, we sat down for a couple of hours to discuss the task at hand. We were faced with something new. The goal was to make the experience better, to make shopping for sweets a better experience for the customers. As mentioned, this was all new to us, previously we re-designed pages, created something new from scratch the usual things, so we took it upon ourselves to create the best experience possible, but we knew the extra hours were necessary.

user interviews

We were delt with the task to make an already good experience better. We had to expand our vision and try to make the user’s experience as smooth and flawless as possible. We used the demo provided from the client to test the experience of shopping for sweets and try to adjust it and make it more intuitive, with our input of course.

analytics

The Analytics provided us with information regarding the design. Overall, similar companies have a somewhat better experience when shopping for sweets & gifts and a mobile first design approach was needed and the desktop version also needed some adjustments.

solution

From the competitor's analysis, user interviews, target groups we came to the conclusion that people like a smooth experience in which they feel engaged until the end, therefore we were not keen on one solution to the problem, so we made a couple regarding mobile & desktop respectfully.

outcome

Once we finished with the discovery phase in the design, we started with low fidelity wireframing, everyone shared their ideas and we concluded the final outcome. After that we chose worked on combining the colors & fonts all together so that they can work in unison. We started with the mobile version first, we could not conclude one solution so we made a couple of them also for the desktop version. Finally, we worked on the animations & transitions. This step was key to make the user experience flawless and make the user feel engaged throughout the whole process.

learnings

This project touched on a realm that we never experienced & worked on before during our time in the academy. We were forced in a way to think of “out of the box” solutions and try to make an already good experience better. The challenge was great and we worked as a team on new levels and experienced a whole new level of design. If another opportunity like this comes around again, I will take it without hesitation, because a lot of things can be learned when not in the comfort zone, per say.
