project

talent market

summary

A redesign of an existing page. A fresh new look
and more oriented towards the users.

role
ux/ui designer

problem definition

Naturally the project started with a brief session from our client. He explained to us his goal and vision, and also explained his blockers and issues regarding the project. We then asked additional questions in order to gain further information and clarify the whole process.

research

After the briefing session, we conducted that the user wanted to a whole re-design of the existing page, also we determined the must have functionalities. This page is a personalized URL, which the client sends, after the company in question sends them a request what they expect from a candidate. The clients additionally want to send another URL, once every 3-6 months, with exceptional students called The Super Talent Market, in which the best students will be placed, during their run in the academy.

user interviews

We started with analyzing the existing page, and figuring out the obstacles that we need to work out and solve. The main problem with the existing page was the navigation, the logo was too big and the student cards needed some cleaning up. I researched the competitors and did a detailed competitive analysis. From the research I concluded that we need to focus on a cleaner and easier to use design, given the fact that the main target group I.e., HR people have pretty busy schedules and would love to find the required information quickly.

analytics

The Analytics showed that a mobile first design was not needed, given the nature of the product and its main target group. Also, from the research I conducted that the student cards need to be clear and to highlight the key traits of every student.

solution

After the analysis of the competitors, user interviews, target groups the solution would be to redesign the existing website, make it intuitive and user-friendly, and make it as easy to use as possible.

outcome

After the research we firstly finished with the wireframes, after that we choose the color palette for the Brainster Talent market & Super Talent market pages respectively. For the Talent Market we created a more cartoonish design, while the Super Talent Market has a more serious approach, giving out the vibe “here are the best of the best”. We kept the clean look of the cards so the HR people can easily see & access all the needed information about the candidates. We gave the Super Talent market a black and gold colorway with textures in the background because gold associates with royalty.

learnings

Gained more experience as a person and as a UX/UI designer. I’ve learned a new dimension in working with a team and trying to balance the workload with the team. Overall, a great experience.
